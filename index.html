About Me

I've always had a passion for creating things from scratch. Growing up, I always enjoyed building my own creations with K'nex and Legos and be so proud of my work, and then video games came. I spent most of my teen years at my computer or on my playstation being amazed at what can be accomplished with technology.

After years of different types of work, I found myself not facing challenges anymore, and anything I accomplished wasn't my creation. I wanted to finally push myself to pursue my dream of becoming a developer.

On my free time outside of work, I looked into courses online like udemy. I have never been so excited to get home and learn something new each day. Being able to see my code turn into the something on the screen was exhilarating.

I realized that learning something on your own is helpful, but you won't get the full experience of what a professional could teach you in person.
